Blogtober

A blogging initiative for the month of October

Join a community of Twitter users in resurrecting your writing.

Share your posts using the hashtag #Blogtober.

1. Start a Blog

While any service is allowed, we recommend Medium for those who want to focus on the words, and a href="https://wordpress.com/">WordPress for those who want to add other media.

2. Update at Least Once a Week

You don't have to write your magnum opus.

Write one post once a week and share it.
